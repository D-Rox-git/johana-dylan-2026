# Photos

Drop your own photos in here named `1.jpg`, `2.jpg`, `3.jpg`, `4.jpg`
(landscape works best). They appear automatically in the "Discover
Serre Chevalier" gallery on `infos.html`.

- Any missing slot hides itself — no broken images.
- Want more than 4? Add `<img src="assets/photos/5.jpg" ... onerror="this.remove()">`
  lines in `infos.html` (the `#gallery` div).
- Resize to ~1200px wide and compress (e.g. squoosh.app) so pages stay fast.
- Good sources: your own snaps, or ask the auberge for a few press photos
  you're allowed to reuse. Avoid copying random images off the web.
